About Me

Hello! I'm Kene, a PhD candidate in epidemiology at the University of Geneva and co‑founder of The GRAPH Courses. I build data‑driven solutions and teach data science, specializing in R, Python and JavaScript.

My research focuses on infectious‑disease modelling and improving health outcomes for vulnerable populations. I love leveraging Generative AI to automate tasks, visualise insights, and communicate science more effectively.
